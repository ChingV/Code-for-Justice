HOUSING FOR IMMIGRANTS

Image

NC Housing Resources

The United States is experiencing a housing crisis. This means there are not enough homes for individuals and families in the USA. It has caused home prices to increase. This makes it hard for new immigrants to find an affordable place to live. Below are a few resources to help find housing.


HUD

Image

HUD Resources

HUD-funded programs that provide emergency shelter and transitional housing (for up to two years) are available to all immigrants, regardless of immigration status, so long as these programs do not consider in- come in determining eligibility. For more information regarding HUD click the link down below.


USA Hello Partnership

Image

USA Hello

Another great resource for finding housing is our partner company USA Hello. USA Hello is a non-profit organization that connects immigrant communities with the information and resources they need to thrive on a national scale. On USA Hello link below you can find housing and many other resources (housing, food, healthcare, and much more.) in your area.
